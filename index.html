Haoyue Tang

 

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently a postdoctoral associate in the Institute of Network Science, Yale University, advised by Prof. Leandros Tassiulas.

I received my PhD and my Bachelor degrees in the Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University in 2022 and 2017, respectively. My advisor is Prof. Jintao Wang. During my PhD studies, I was a visiting student in Telecom Paris, Microsoft Research Asia and Tencent Inc.

Research Interests

My research lies in the broad area of applied probability, stochastic systems and machine learning, with applications in resource orchestration in 6G wireless networks, O-RAN networks, spectrum networks, VR/AR and privacy-preserving data analytics for machine learning systems.
